Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

You can create an account by clicking "Sign Up" on the homepage and entering your email, a secure password, and basic personal information.

How does receipt scanning work?

Use the "Scan Receipt" feature in the app to upload your receipts. The system will extract prices and add them to your budget tracker.

Can I set savings goals?

Yes! Go to the "Savings Goals" section, enter a goal amount and target date, and track your progress easily.

What should I do if I forget my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password via email.
